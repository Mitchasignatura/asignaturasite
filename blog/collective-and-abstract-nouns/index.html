    Collective and Abstract Nouns

    Maria Cecilia Castroverde

    2020-04-12 13:28

    Collective Nouns

    Collective Nouns – denotes a number of persons or things considered as a group or a whole

    PEOPLE  ANIMALS  THINGS/IDEA 
    A troupe of performers A plague of locust  A hand of bananas
     A tribe of natives  A nest of mice  A wealth of information
     A crew of sailors  A farrow of piglets  A book of stamps
    A house of senators  A drove of pigs  A cache of jewels
    A bevy of ladies  A gaze of raccoons  A ring of keys

    Abstract Nouns

    Abstract Nouns – exact opposite of concrete nouns because they cannot be perceived by the senses.

    IDEAS  QUALITIES   EMOTIONS CONDITIONS 
     Abolition  Gentleness  Happiness  Richness
     Nationalism  Integrity  Cheerfulness  Poverty
     Militarism  Strength  Gladness  Health
     Evolution  Humility  Shock  Sickness
